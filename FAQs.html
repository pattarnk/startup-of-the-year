FAQs

Who are Life Food?

Life Food are a prep meal company like no other. We realized that there was a huge gap in the market for a meal prep company that was not only affordable but truly bespoke.

We aren’t here to skimp on portions or make a huge mark-up whilst our customers go hungry. Our meals will leave you feeling full and fuelled, ready to take on everything life has to throw at you.

Bringing quality at a fantastic price, using fresh ingredients and locally sourced meat, we are sure you will love our meals.

How Does It Work?

Simply select your combination from our mouth-watering menu, indicate what weights you would like* to suit your macros and let our chefs take care of the rest for you. It really is that simple!

How Much Does It Cost?

The price per meal depends on the weights you select. Our Muscle Gain meals (200g of Protein Source, 200g of Carbohydrate Source and 30g of Vegetables) cost just £7.50 per meal. Plus, we won’t charge you for delivery… that’s on us*.

*Extra delivery charges will apply for any orders placed for weekend delivery.

How Do The Deliveries Work?

We will typically have your meals to you in 48 hours from you ordering, if ordering before 2pm, for example:

  • Orders placed Monday will arrive Wednesday.
  • Orders placed Tuesday will arrive Thursday.
  • Orders placed Wednesday will arrive Friday.
  • Orders placed Thursday will arrive the following Tuesday.

So Orders placed Monday to Wednesday, will arrive 48 hours later. Any orders Thursday onwards will be dispatched for the following week.

We use DPD as our delivery company and they will deliver nationwide for us. Your meals will be sealed in temperature-controlled packaging to keep it cool and protected all day. Then DPD will give you a one-hour time slot for your delivery the morning / afternoon of delivery. If you’re not going to be home, you’ll be able to let them know if they need to leave with a neighbour or in a safe place.

How Long Do Your Meals Last?

Our meals will stay fresh in the fridge for 3 days. If you wanted to freeze your meals, we recommend you do so for no longer than 28 days. We also recommend that you de-frost before re-heating… as no one likes frozen broccoli!

How Do I Heat My Meals?

All of our meals are packed into microwavable containers (which you can wash, and re-use should you wish) and we recommend between 3-4 minutes in a conventional microwave oven.
